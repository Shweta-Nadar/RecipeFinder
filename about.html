Our Story

Welcome to Hack & Snack! 🍽️💻
A recipe finder built for coders, by coders!


Ever struggled to decide what to cook while debugging your latest LeetCode challenge? We did too! That’s why we built CodeBites, a smart recipe finder that helps programmers quickly find delicious meals with minimal effort.

We believe writing clean code and eating good food go hand in hand. Our goal is to provide quick, easy, and brain-boosting recipes that keep coders fueled while they solve tough problems.

Our Mission

🍽️

Inspire Cooking

Inspiring coders to cook with efficiency, creativity, and a dash of algorithmic precision!

🌍

Celebrate Diversity

Just like coding embraces every language, we celebrate the rich diversity of global cuisines. From classic comfort foods to exotic flavors, there's a recipe for everyone!

🥗

Promote Healthy Eating

Just like clean code makes a program efficient, healthy eating fuels a better life. We promote balanced, nutritious meals without compromising on taste!

Just like developers collaborate to solve problems, we bring food lovers together to share, learn, and grow.
