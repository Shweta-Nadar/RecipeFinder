Chocolate Brownies

Rich and fudgy chocolate brownies that are perfect for any occasion

Home Categories Popular Recipes About
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Total Time: 40 mins
Servings: 16 brownies
Dessert Baking Sweet Chocolate Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval until completely melted.

  3. Add the sugar to the melted butter and whisk until well combined.

  4. Add the eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.

  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Do not overmix.

  7. Fold in the chocolate chips and nuts (if using).

  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).

  10. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.

  11. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the brownies out of the pan. Cut into 16 squares and serve.

Notes

  • For extra fudgy brownies, you can refrigerate them for a few hours before serving.
  • Don't overbake! Brownies should be slightly underbaked for that perfect fudgy texture.
  • For a flavor variation, add 1/2 teaspoon of espresso powder to enhance the chocolate flavor.
  • These brownies freeze well for up to 3 months. Wrap individual pieces in plastic wrap and store in a freezer bag.
