Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil

Home Categories Popular Recipes About
Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 2 (10-inch pizzas)
Italian Dinner Vegetarian Pizza Baking

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

For the Topping:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

For the Pizza Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. Add olive oil and warm water. Stir until a dough forms.

  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions. Let rest for 10 minutes.

For Assembly and Baking:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

  2. Roll out each portion of dough on a floured surface to form two 10-inch circles.

  3. In a small bowl, mix the tomato sauce with minced garlic and a tablespoon of olive oil.

  4. Spread the sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  5. Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce.

  6. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and dried oregano if using.

  7. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  8. Remove from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves, a drizzle of olive oil, and grated Parmesan if desired.

  9. Slice and serve hot.

Notes

  • For the best flavor, use high-quality ingredients, especially the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  • If you don't have time to make dough from scratch, you can use store-bought pizza dough.
  • A pizza stone helps achieve a crispy crust, but a regular baking sheet works well too.
  • Add the fresh basil after baking to preserve its flavor and color.
